The Bridgewater-Raritan Coding and Robotics Exposition

The Bridgewater-Raritan Coding and Robotics Exposition is an event that will be held at Bridgewater-Raritan Middle School, on Saturday, April 13th, 2024. It is a great way for attendees to get interested in computer science, programming, and robotics. The Bridgewater-Rartian Computer Science Club (BRCS) will team up with the Robotics Club, and BRHS's Team 303 to help make this event possible. We plan to have Python, Java, C++, Robotics Competitions, Q, Microbit, and other events and workshops available to attendees. These workshops will be taught by BRCS Leaders and students at BRMS. Amongst the leaders are distinguished programmers and hackathon winners Alex Sheng, Dhruv Rakhade, and Tharun Naguleswaren. We hope to see you at this event, and we there will be free food (breakfast), and we are discussing prizes!

Details

- Location: 128 Merriwood Rd, Bridgewater, NJ 08807

- Time: 9:00 AM - 12:00 PM, 4/13/2024

- Cost to Attend: Free!

FAQ

Q: How can I register?

A: You can just walk in! (For BRCS Members - If you want points / credit for showing up please fill out the Google Form attached on Classroom)


Q: What topics will there be? Like specifically?

A: Alex Sheng and Shubham Roy-Choudhury will cover Python, Dhruv Rakhade and Charlie Yang will cover C++ and Arduinos, Jay Fang will cover Java, Samir Rangwalla will cover AI, some select 8th graders will cover Q Robot/Coding, some select 7th graders will cover Microcode, the Robotics club will show off their creations, and Team 303 will have a Robotics Competition.


Q: What's in it for me?

A: The learning experience! (We have free food and prizes too! You'll have to be there to find out more about that though.)


Q: Do I need to know how to code?

A: Nope! We will introduce you to coding if you are a complete beginner. Our top hackathon team will also help develop your skills if you are a intermediate or advanced coder


Q: If I am a BRCS member, should I show up?

A: Yup! If you show up, the leadership and advisor team has prepared a complete Arduino programming kit, raffled off to those who filled out the form and attend the BRMS Coding and Robotics Exposition!
