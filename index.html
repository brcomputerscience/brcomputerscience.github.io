About Us

The Bridgewater-Raritan Computer Science Club (BRCS) is a student-led club that focuses on computer science. We meet after school on specific days and offer opportunities to enhance students' computer science knowledge. We have sub-groups in various fields, from AI to computer engineering, catering to all skill levels. Whether you're a novice or an experienced coder, there's something here for everyone. Join us to socialize with like-minded individuals passionate about computer science.
