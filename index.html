About Us

BRCS, in short, is a computer science-based student-led club. We meet after school on (x days) and have opportunities to enhance students’ computer-science knowledge. We have sub-groups in different fields ranging from AI to computer engineering, all of which can develop students’ skills. These groups are open to all difficulty levels, from people that have never touched a computer besides the school’s or ones that have been coding their whole life. There will always be something that fits for every individual. In addition, this club gives students a chance to socialize with others who are passionate about computer science, which prior to this club would be a rare occasion.
