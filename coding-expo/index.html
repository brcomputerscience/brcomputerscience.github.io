The Bridgewater-Raritan Coding and Robotics Exposition

The Bridgewater-Raritan Coding and Robotics Exposition is a event held at Bridgewater-Raritan Middle School, on TBD. It is a great way for young children to get interested in computer science, programming, and robotics. The Bridgewater-Rartian Computer Science Club (BRCS) will team up with the Robotics Club to help make this event possible. We are still finalizing details, but we plan to have Python, Java, C++, Robotics, EV3, and other workshops available to attendees. These workshops will be taught by the BRCS Leaders, who are students at BRMS. Amongst the leaders are Hackathon and Coding competition winners Alex Sheng, Dhruv Rakhade, and Tharun Naguleswaren. We hope to see you at this event, and we (might) provide free food and drinks, so stay tuned!
